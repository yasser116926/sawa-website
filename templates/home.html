About SAWA

Founded in 2019 as the Sudan Music Research Center and re-registered in Uganda in 2025, SPJ empowers artists and communities impacted by conflict. We advance democratic values, nonviolence, and cultural initiatives that foster reconciliation and protect vulnerable groups.

Our Projects

SPJ's projects include artist grants, residencies, capacity-building workshops, trauma recovery through arts, advocacy campaigns, and cross-border cultural collaborations connecting Sudanese and international artists.
